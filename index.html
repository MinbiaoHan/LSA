Learning Seminar for Strategic Learning

About the Seminar

This seminar aims to bring researchers interested in strategic machine learning to learn the state-of-the-art results in this space together. Each seminar features one invited expert and the talk is expected to cover one or two papers in detail and present the concrete techniques.

Organization

The seminar is organized by a group of researchers who are fascinated by machine learning questions in presence of strategic agent behaviors. We are hoping to invite experts in this field to deliver technical lectures so that the audience can learn the state of the art results and techniques. We also hope this could be a platform for communicating and discussing research findings from all relevant areas, and hopeful sparking new ideas and collaborations.

Schedule
